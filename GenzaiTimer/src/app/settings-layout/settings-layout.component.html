Duration

Control the duration of work time and break time.

Work

Amount of time (in minutes) you'd like to spend on your work.

Amount of time (in minutes) you'd like to spend on your short break.

Long Break

Amount of time (in minutes) you'd like to spend on your long Break.

Interval

Long Break Interval

Amount of short breaks you'd like to take before taking a longer break.
